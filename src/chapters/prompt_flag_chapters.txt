### Goal ###

You must classify scenes depicted in a video section of a historic German episode of Deutsche Wochenschau.
To do this, you must read the German chapter description and German audio transcription thoroughly

### Classes ###

is_war_report: The scene primarily focuses on reporting on the ongoing war and German troops are involved.
is_combat_scene: The scene shows actual combat with German participation (staged or re-inacted does count into it). Scenes like this depict German weapons and/or German soldiers in action. Combat scenes without German involvement do NOT count.
german_soldiers_depicted: The scene shows German soldiers. This can be combat or interaction with civilians or marches or anything more.

### Chapter Description ###

$chapter_description$

### Audio Transcription ###

$audio_transcription$

### Result format ###

The result must be a complete comma-separated CSV including the header.
You must insert your boolean values inside the following CSV and return the completed CSV.
Inserted values must be either True or False (Python booleans with first letter capitalized)

episode_id,chapter,is_war_report,is_combat_scene,german_soldiers_depicted
$filestem$,$chapter$,INSERT_IS_WAR_REPORT as bool,INSERT_IS_COMBAT_SCENE as bool, INSERT_GERMAN_SOLDIERS_DEPICTED as bool,
